The Revolutionary Tour

Currently only available on demand.

During this expedition, Jupiter introduces you to symbolic locations that deal with the political (counter)revolutions that took place in the last 200 years. A convincing analysis of German politics is the thread in this tour, while experimental thoughts and anecdotes can make this expedition more playful. In the meanwhile, you'll be surprised that even in the nearby history – the stage of historical events that changed the world can already be forgotten.

Length: ±3km // ±4 hours

Costs: Younger than 18 or student: €8.00 // Adult: €14.

Meeting point: in front of the Hopfingerbräu im Palais (Ebertstrasse 24)

End point: S-Bahnhof Bornholmer Strasse

Public transportation ticket (Berlin AB) required.
