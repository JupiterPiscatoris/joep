About Jupiter

Jupiter Piscatoris (alias of Joep de Visser, born 1989 - Holland) was charmed by history at a young age. For him, Berlin turned out to be the right place to settle down. After a MA in German History, Jupiter was devoted to his blog about locations of historical value in ‘the Capital of the Twentieth Century’. With this project, Jupiter gained an immense knowledge about the German capital. In the meanwhile, he has been active for several media and platforms (Exberliner, Wyme, Slow Travel Berlin) and the celebrated Berlin correspondent for the Dutch ‘Jonge Historici’ (Young Historians).  

With Urban Berlin Tours, Jupiter offers to share his insight for the German capital. In addition to his specialisation in the history of Nazi-Germany, he knows about a variety of topics including dozens of memorials, interesting architecture, historical subcultures and of course: Berlin now & the development in the nearby future.
