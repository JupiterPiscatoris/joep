Victims of the Nazis

Currently only available on demand.

In this fascinating expedition, Jupiter brings you to seven memorials where victims of Nazism are being remembered. Besides giving an insight of the intolerant ideology of the Nazi's, the tour deals with sensitive themes such as remembrance and victimship. Among the seven memorials, the Memorial for the Murdered Jews of Europe ('Holocaust Memorial') stands somewhat central. This memorial has a high level of abstraction which needs to be explained in a competent way. The tour has an 'open end' at a fairly undiscovered museum.

Length: ±3km // ±3,5 hours

Costs: Younger than 18 or student: €7.50 // Adult: €12.

Meeting point: Platform of U Bundestag U-bahn(subway) station
